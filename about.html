About VCCA

Shaping a more professional, connected, and identity-driven cricket culture in Varanasi.

VCCA was founded to give working professionals, institutions, departments, and corporate teams a trusted platform to compete with pride, play with integrity, and grow stronger through sport. We bring structure, accountability, and community spirit to every match and every tournament.

Our Story

Origins and purpose

VCCA was created to bring professionals and organizations together through sport, mentorship, and healthy competition. We believe cricket should be played with identity, dignity, and fairness at its core.

Why VCCA

Transparency & identity

Mission

Our approach

Vision

Our future

Values that guide us

What makes our platform stand apart

Fair Competition

We believe sport should be played with respect, discipline, and strong sportsmanship from the first ball to the final over.

One Identity

Each team represents a real organization, department, institution, or professional group, keeping the competition authentic and meaningful.

Professional Standards

We maintain transparent governance, clear tournament formats, and a culture of accountability at every level.

Community Impact

VCCA helps create stronger relationships between colleagues, peers, and local communities through a shared sporting experience.
